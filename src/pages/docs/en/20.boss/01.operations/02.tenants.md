## Overview

Tenant governance sits between platform identity and runtime consumption. After a tenant exists in IAM, administrators use Rune Admin to assign quotas, approved flavors, and workspace-level operating boundaries.

## Governance Focus

- Keep resource allocation aligned with business owners and tenant administrators.
- Allocate quotas by cluster and flavor rather than by raw hardware assumptions.
- Review workspace-level distribution after tenant-level quotas are updated.
- Use dashboards and usage views to decide when a tenant needs more or less capacity.
