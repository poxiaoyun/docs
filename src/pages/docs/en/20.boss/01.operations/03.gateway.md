## Overview

The gateway workflow begins when a model-serving endpoint needs to be shared with a wider audience. Boss administrators validate the channel configuration, apply safety and access policies, and track usage after release.

## Key Steps

- Register or expose the target service through the gateway.
- Issue API keys and limit who can call the service.
- Apply moderation and routing policies before opening broader access.
- Use audit and operations pages to watch volume, failures, and policy violations after release.
