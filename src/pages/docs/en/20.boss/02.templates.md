## Overview

System templates are the controlled building blocks that Boss makes available to Rune users. They usually package runtime defaults, image choices, volumes, and policy-safe parameters into reusable deployment entries.

## Lifecycle

- Author the template according to the platform schema and parameter rules.
- Submit it for review with impact scope and rollback information.
- Roll it out gradually to selected tenants or projects before platform-wide release.
- Deprecate old versions once the replacement is proven and migration guidance is ready.
