## Overview

The license page helps administrators confirm whether the current platform entitlement is healthy and whether any action is needed before expiration affects product behavior.

## What to Watch

- Check the current license state, effective range, and expiration time.
- Use warning states to begin renewal work before any feature restrictions appear.
- Coordinate with the platform provider and validate the updated entitlement after maintenance.
- Treat expiry as a shared risk across Boss, Rune, Moha, and ChatApp rather than a product-local issue.
