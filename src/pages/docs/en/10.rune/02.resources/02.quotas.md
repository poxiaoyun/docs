## Overview

Quota policy in Rune is hierarchical. Platform administrators allocate resources to a tenant first, then tenant administrators divide that budget across one or more workspaces. Users consume resources inside the workspace boundary.

## What to Check

- Review tenant-level limits when a workspace cannot receive additional resources.
- Review workspace allocations before deploying or resizing workloads.
- Track quota usage together with running workloads to understand why new scheduling requests are blocked.
- Coordinate with administrators when new flavors appear but no usable quota has been assigned to them.
