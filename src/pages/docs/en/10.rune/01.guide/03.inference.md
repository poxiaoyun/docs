## Overview

Inference hosting is the common delivery target for trained or tuned models. In Rune, a model is deployed from a template into a workspace, monitored as an instance, and optionally registered to the gateway for broader consumption.

## What to Understand

- Deployment starts from the [Inference Services](/rune/console/inference) module or from a matching template in the App Market.
- Flavor choice affects performance, parallelism, and cost boundaries.
- Logs, monitoring panels, and Kubernetes events are the main troubleshooting surfaces after deployment.
- Gateway registration turns a healthy inference instance into a managed service entry for other clients and teams.
