## Overview

Before you deploy anything in Rune, confirm that the administrator has already assigned a tenant, connected a cluster, created a workspace, and allocated enough quota for your workload type.

## Checklist

- Confirm you can sign in and switch to the correct tenant and workspace.
- Check that the current workspace has enough quota for CPU, memory, GPU, and storage.
- Verify that at least one template and one flavor are visible in the target cluster.
- Prepare model files, datasets, or container images if your workload depends on them.
