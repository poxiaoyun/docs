## Account settings

1. Click the avatar in the upper right corner to enter "Personal Information".
2. Set up email and security policies and enable two-factor login.
3. Configure Notification subscription to ensure immediate notification of workspace updates.

## Teamwork

- Invite members to join the organization and set role permissions.
- Bind enterprise identity providers (such as DingTalk, GitHub) to complete single sign-on.
- Configure resource quotas and access domains for each team.
