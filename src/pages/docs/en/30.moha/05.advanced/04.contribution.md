## Overview

Participate in open source projects and community events to connect with AI developers around the world.

## How to contribute

### Share models and datasets

- Upload high-quality models
- Share training data sets
-Write detailed documentation

### Participate in open source projects

- Fork the project and improve it
- Submit Pull Request
- Bug fixes and features added

### Write a tutorial

- Share your experience
- Write technical blogs
- Record video tutorials

### answer the questions

- Answer questions in the forum
- Help newbies solve problems
- Share best practices

## Contribution rewards

### Points system

- Upload model: +50 points
- Answer the question: +10 points
- Write a tutorial: +100 points
- Participate in open source: +200 points

### Badge of Honor

- **Rising Star Contributor**: First time contributor
- **Active Developer**: Contribute continuously for 3 months
- **Model Expert**: Upload 10+ models
- **Community Star**: Get 100+ likes
