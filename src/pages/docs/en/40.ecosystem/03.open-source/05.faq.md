## FAQ

### Why do we need to do a version matrix first?

Because there is a strong binding relationship between hardware driver/runtime and framework dependencies, determining the matrix first can significantly reduce troubleshooting costs.

### Why is there "Containers and Orchestration/Observables/Artifact Security" in the directory?

These contents have nothing to do with the specific hardware ecology, but have the greatest impact on delivery and stability, and are suitable for being precipitated into reusable general practices.
