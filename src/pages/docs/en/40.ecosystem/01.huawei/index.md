## Overview

This chapter focuses on the delivery and adaptation of Huawei's Ascend ecosystem on the cluster/node side: drivers and runtime, as well as related installation preparations for MindCluster (cluster scheduling component).

:::tip
It is recommended to clarify three things first: **hardware model**, **driver/runtime version**, and **framework version**. Subsequent troubleshooting and compatibility judgment rely on these three.
:::
